Auto logbooks loans

At Kawa Credit, you can borrow up to 50% the value of your car and get the funds in 12 hours only. The interest is as low as 5% per month and loan life is 12 months maximum.

General Requirements

  • Personal or Business Bank Statements for the last 6 months
  • Copy of National ID or Passport
  • Copy or KRA PIN of registered owner of the vehicle
  • Copy of motor vehicle records with KRA
  • A Cheque Book Account
  • Motor Vehicle Evaluation (Done at our premise) and Photograph
  • Motor Vehicle Logbook
  • 2 Blank but signed Motor Vehicle Ownership Transfer Forms
  • Execution of Chattels Mortgage (Done at our Office)
  • 2 Passport Photos of the applicant
  • Documents to show proof of Comprehensive Motor Vehicle Insurance
  • Legal Charges (To be advised at our office according to loan amount)
  • Payslips for the last 3 Months (Employed Persons)
  • Copy of Employment Contract or Job ID (Employed Persons)

Person Loans

Kawa Credit enters into check-off arrangement with public and private sector employers to enable their employees to access short and medium terms credit.

Features

  • Be in employment for a minimum of 6months
  • No collateral required
  • Deduction at source to secure repayments
  • Affordability as per pay slip
  • Clean credit history at the CRB
